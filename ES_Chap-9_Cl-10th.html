Chapter-9

Role and Importance of Entrepreneurship


1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Tick(✓) the correct answer.

1. Which of these is not a characteristic of a successful entrepreneur?
a. Risk taking
b. Determination
c. Nature lover
d. Innovativeness
✅ Answer: c. Nature lover
---
2. Which of these is the main aim of entrepreneurship?
a. Earning profit
b. Providing innovative products and services c. Both (a) and (b)
d. Neither (a) nor (b)
✅ Answer: c. Both (a) and (b)
---
3. Which of these is a disadvantage of entrepreneurship as a career?
a. Freedom of work
b. Uncertain earnings
c. Independence
d. Excitement
✅ Answer: b. Uncertain earnings
---
4. Which of these is true about entrepreneurship?
a. A lot of money is needed to start an entrepreneurial venture.
b. Entrepreneurial ventures are mostly successful.
c. Entrepreneurial ventures are easy to start.
d. Most of the entrepreneurial ventures end up as failures.
✅ Answer: d. Most of the entrepreneurial ventures end up as failures.
---
5. Which of the following are functions of entrepreneurs?
a. Hiring people
b. Innovation
c. Motivation
d. All of these
✅ Answer: d. All of these
---
6. Sam is the owner of a multi brand store. A customer comes to his store and starts shouting at him. He does not get angry. He listens to what he is saying. What quality do you think he has?
a. Hardworking
b. Confident
c. Patient
d. Risk bearing
✅ Answer: c. Patient
---
