Role and Importance of Entrepreneurship


1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Tick(✓) the correct answer.

1. Which of these is not a characteristic of a successful entrepreneur?
a. Risk taking
b. Determination
c. Nature lover
d. Innovativeness

2. Which of these is the main aim of entrepreneurship?
a. Earning profit
b. Providing innovative products and services c. Both (a) and (b)

3. Which of these is a disadvantage of entrepreneurship as a career?

III. Short Answer Questions.

1. What is meant by an entrepreneur? What is entrepreneurship?
Answer:
Entrepreneur is a person who undertakes entrepreneurship by launching and overseeing the operations of a new business venture.

3. State some functions that an entrepreneur performs for his enterprise.
Answer:
Functions of entrepreneur-
1.Identifying entrepreneurial opportunity:
There are many opportunities in the world of business.An entrepreneur senses the opportunities faster than others do. And keeping his eyes and ears open, he applies his imagination, creativity and innovativeness to attain business success.
2.Turning ideas into action:
An entrepreneur brings about innovative ideas, products and services and packages them to suit the demands of the market.
3.Feasibility study:
An entrepreneur conducts studies to assess the feasibility of the market as regards the proposed products or services.
4.Resourcing:
The entrepreneur ensures the availability of various resources like money, machine, material and manpower needed to run the enterprise successfully.
---
4. How is an entrepreneur different from a regular businessman?
Answer:
An entrepreneur is different from a regular businessman. A businessman is someone who sets up a business with an existing idea offering products and services to customers. An entrepreneur, on the other hand, is a person who starts an enterprise with a new idea or concept or a better way to offer an existing idea or concept.
---
5. Write some advantages of entrepreneurship over wage employment.
Answer:
Advantages of Entrepreneurship-
1. It gives freedom:
If you are working for a boss and a company, then, you have little freedom on the job. On the other hand, if you start your own business, you will be able to keep your own hours and set your own tasks and schedule. This gives you a level of freedom that you will not find when you are employed.
2. It can be exciting:
Entrepreneurship can be very exciting. Every day will be filled with new opportunities to challenge your determination, skills and abilities.
3. It allows you to set your own earnings:
It allows you to set your own wages and earnings. The work that you do would be for something you own.
4. It offers flexibility:
As an entrepreneur, you can schedule your work hours around other commitments, including spending quality time with your family.
---
6. What are some myths about entrepreneurship?
Answer:
Some myths about entrepreneurship are-
1. Every business idea needs to be unique or special .
2. A lot of money is needed to start up a new business .
3. Only a person having a big business is an entrepreneur .
4. Entrepreneurs are born, not made.
