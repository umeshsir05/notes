III. Short Answer Questions.

1. What do you understand by the Frame style in LibreOffice Writer?
Answer-
In LibreOffice Writer, Frame Style refers to predefined formatting settings applied to frames—movable containers that hold text, images, or other objects. Frame styles control properties like size, position, borders, background, and text wrapping. They help maintain consistent design throughout the document.

2. What do you understand by Styles in context of the LibreOffice Writer word processor?
Answer-
A style is a set of text formatting characteristics, such as font size, color, and alignment, which can be applied to a paragraph or any selected piece of text in a document. Styles ensure consistency and make formatting easier.
