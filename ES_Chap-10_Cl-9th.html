1. What do you mean by the environment? The environment includes all living and non-living things that surround us and affect our life. 2. What are biotic and abiotic components of the environment? Biotic components: living things (plants, animals, humans). Abiotic components: non-living things (air, water, soil, sunlight). 3. What do you mean by the ecosystem? An ecosystem is a system where living organisms interact with each other and with their physical surroundings. 4. What is environmental degradation? It is the deterioration of the environment due to harmful human activities. 5. Write some measures to protect the environment. Plant trees, reduce pollution, conserve water, use renewable energy, and follow the 4 R’s. 6. What do you mean by sustainable development? Using resources wisely so that future generations can also meet their needs. 7. What do you mean by green skills? Skills that help protect the environment, such as waste management, energy conservation, and eco-friendly practices. 8. What do you mean by green economy? An economy that reduces pollution, uses resources efficiently, and promotes social well-being. 9. What is the importance of green economy? It reduces pollution, conserves resources, supports sustainable development, and creates eco-friendly jobs. 10. Write a short note on the 4 R's. The 4 R’s are Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, and Recover. They help minimize waste and protect the environment. 11. Write the do’s and don’ts to become a green citizen. Do’s: Plant trees, save water, conserve energy, reduce waste. Don’ts: Do not litter, do not waste electricity, do not burn plastic, do not cut trees.
