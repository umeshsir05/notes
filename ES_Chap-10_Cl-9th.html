IV. Short Answer Questions.

1. What do you mean by the environment?
Answer-
Everything that surrounds human beings such as places, people, things, and nature make up there environment. Our environment is a combination of natural things and man-made things.

2. What are biotic and abiotic components of the environment?
Answer-
Human beings, plants, animals, birds, microorganism and other living creatures are examples of the biotic components of the environment.
Land, water, soil, air, sunlight, wind, rocks, mountains and other nonliving things are example of abiotic component of the environment.

3. What do you mean by the ecosystem?
Answer-
An ecosystem is a system where living organisms interact with each other and with their physical surroundings.

4. What is environmental degradation?
Answer-
Environmental degradation is any change or disturbance in the environment that is perceived as undesirable and harmful for it.

5. Write some measures to protect the environment.
Answer-
Measures to protect the environment:
1. Plant more trees and protect existing forests.
2. Reduce the use of plastic and avoid littering.
3. Save water and use it wisely.
4. Reduce pollution by using public transport or carpooling.
5. Conserve electricity by switching off lights and fans when not needed.
6. Follow the 4 R’s: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle, and Recover.
7. Use renewable sources of energy like solar and wind.
8. Keep your surroundings clean and promote cleanliness in the community.

6. What do you mean by sustainable development?
Answer-
Sustainable development is development that meets the needs of the present generations without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their needs.

7. What do you mean by green skills?
Answer-
Green skills refer to those workplace skills that help us to sustain our environment in our lifetime and that of our future generations.

8. What do you mean by green economy?
Answer-
The term 'Green Economy' was first coined in a 1989 report called the 'Blueprint' for a Green Economy. Green economy can be defined as a system which helps in economic growth while at the same time taking care of the environment.
