Chapter – 1

Methods of Communication


I. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Tick() the correct answer.

1. Which of the following is NOT an element of communication process?
a. Channel
b. Receiver
c. Sender
d. Graph
Answer – d. Graph

2. Which method of communication will you use to apply for a two days leave from school?
a. Poster
b. E-mail
c. Application
d. Newsletter
Answer – c. Application

3. What do you think is the purpose of communication?
a. To inform
b. To share thoughts, ideas and feelings
c. To influence others
d. All of these
Answer – d. All of these

4. Which of these is not an element of the nonverbal communication?
a. Words
b. Gestures
c. Expressions
d. Posture
Answer – a. Words

5. Which of these actions a sender cannot use to send his messages?
a. Gestures
b. Speaking
c. Reading
d. Writing
Answer – c. Reading

6. Which of the following is an example of oral communication?
a. Newspapers
b. Letters
c. Phone call
d. E-mail
Answer – c. Phone call

7. What does an upright body posture convey during a conversation?
a. Pride
b. Confidence
c. Humility
d. Diffidence
Answer – b. Confidence

III. Short Answer Questions.

1. What is the meaning of verbal communication?
Answer –
When communication takes place with the help of words (spoken or written), it is referred to as verbal communication. Language plays a very important role.

2. How is verbal communication different from non-verbal communication?
Answer –
Verbal communication uses spoken/written words, while non-verbal uses body language, expressions, gestures, and tone. Verbal = language; Non-verbal = emotions/support.

3. List down a few limitations of non-verbal communication.
Answer –
1. It is quite vague and imprecise.
2. Long conversations or detailed explanations not possible.
3. Sometimes it may be difficult to understand.

4. What is visual communication? Give two examples.
Answer –
Visual communication = transmission of ideas via signs, symbols, photos, tables, drawings, videos. Examples: Charts, Maps.

5. List two limitations of visual communication?
Answer –
1. It can be costly.
2. Complete information cannot always be conveyed.

6. What is paralanguage in non-verbal communication?
Answer –
Paralanguage = nonverbal voice elements: pitch, rhythm, tempo, resonance, pace, fillers like "um", "aah".
