Ability to work Independently


1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Tick(✓) the correct answer.

1. What makes you complete work or studies without others cheering you? a. Self-awareness b. Communication ✅ c. Self-motivation d. Self-esteem

2. Which of these is not a quality that is required by an independently working person? a. Self-motivation ✅ b. Distress c. Self-regulation d. Self-awareness

3. A feeling of self-awareness generally enhances our ____________. a. Oratory skills b. Work skills c. Stress levels ✅ d. Self-confidence

4. General feeling of self-awareness enhances your a. Self-regulation b. Self-motivation c. Self-confidence ✅ d. All of these

5. Rahul worked hard to win best dancer's award at his school’s annual function. What type of motivation is this? a. Internal ✅ b. External c. Specific d. Non-specific

2. Take steps to improve your weaknesses and **leverage ** your strengths.

3. The **internal** motivation comes from within a person, while external motivation comes from outside forces.

4. SMART stands for Specific, **Measurable**, Achievable, Relevant and Time bound.

5. **Self-regulation** is the ability to manage your thoughts, feelings, and behaviour to achieve your goals.
