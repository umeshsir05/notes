III. Short Answer Questions.

Here are the correct and clear answers with
at the end of every line: --- III. Short Answer Questions.

1. Why is self-awareness important in our life?
Answer-
Self-awareness is important because it helps us understand our strengths, weaknesses, emotions, and behavior, allowing us to make better decisions and improve ourselves.

2. What is the exact meaning of self-regulation?
Answer-
Self-regulation means the ability to control one’s thoughts, emotions, and actions to achieve goals and maintain positive behavior.

3. List any two differences between the interests and abilities.
Answer-
1. Interests are what we like to do, while abilities are what we can do well.
2. Interests can change over time, but abilities usually remain constant or improve with practice.

4. What is internal motivation? How is it different from the external motivation?
Answer-
Internal motivation comes from within a person, driven by personal satisfaction or desire to learn.
External motivation comes from outside sources such as rewards, praise, or recognition.

5. List any three qualities of a self-motivated person.
Answer-
1. Self-confidence
2. Determination
3. Positive attitude

6. Give one example of a measurable and non-measurable goal.
Answer-
Measurable goal: Scoring 90% in exams.
Non-measurable goal: Becoming more confident.
