III. Short Answer Questions.


1. Why is self-awareness important in our life?
Answer-
Self-awareness involves recognizing your strength and weakness, your values and beliefs, and how you impact others. It may help you in transforming your weakness into strength and strength into an exceptional ability.

2. What is the exact meaning of self-regulation?
Answer-
Self-regulation is the ability to manage your thoughts, feelings, and behaviour to achieve your goals . It involves setting goals, monitoring progress, adjusting behaviour and using feedback to reduce/ increase the gap between current and desired state.

3. List any two differences between the interests and abilities.
Answer-
1. Interest refers to a person's liking towards a particular subject, activity or hobby, while abilities refers to a person’s innat or acquired skills, talents or capabilities in a particular area.
2. Interests can change over time, while abilities may remain constant are improve with practice and training.

4. What is internal motivation? How is it different from the external motivation?
Answer-
Internal motivation is seen when we do things for our own sake without any sort of external reward only because they make us happy, healthy, and feel good.
External motivation comes from outside sources such as rewards, praise, or recognition.

5. List any three qualities of a self-motivated person.
Answer-
1. Determination
2. Focus
3. Resilient

6. Give one example of a measurable and non-measurable goal.
Answer-
Measurable goal: My goal is to run 1 km in 10 minutes.
Non-measurable goal: I want to run fast.
