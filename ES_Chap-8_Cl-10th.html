III. Short Answer Questions.

1.What do you mean by computer maintenance?
Answer-
Computer maintenance is the practice of keeping the computer in a good working condition by taking care of it.
2.Write some ways to clean a computer keyboard.
Answer-
Before cleaning a keyboard we should unplug it from the computer. You can use a cotton swab or soft brush to remove crumbs and dust particles from the keyboard. Vacuum cleaning brush or a blower can be used to remove dust and crumbs that get stuck between the keys. Do not use water for cleaning as it can damage the keyboard. You however can use isopropyl alcohol for cleaning, as it dries off very quickly.

3.Write some ways to clean a computer mouse.
Answer-
To clean dust and dirt from a mouse use a soft brush. To remove stubborn stains, gently wipe the outer surface, scroll wheel, and optical sensor with a cotton swab dampened with isopropyl alcohol. Be careful not to get any liquid inside the mouse itself.

4.Write some ways to clean a computer monitor.
Answer-
First, power off the monitor and unplug it before cleaning. You can wipe the screen with a soft microfiber cloth to remove any finger marks, dust and smudges. For stubborn stains, lightly dampen the cloth with isopropyl alcohol and use it to wipe the screen.
