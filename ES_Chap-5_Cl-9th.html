IV. Short Answer Questions:
  1. What do you mean by ICTs?
    Answer: The term ICT (Information and Communication Technology) refers to a diverse set of technologies, tools, and resources that help us create, store, manage, and share information.
  2. What is the use of ICTs in the education field?
    Answer: ICT enhances education by making classes interactive and enjoyable. It uses media like images and videos to boost students' memory and understanding. ICT tools simplify complex topics for teachers and offer remote access to abundant learning resources anytime, anywhere.
  3. How do ICTs help in the healthcare sector?
    Answer: ICT has improved healthcare by enabling efficient communication, remote consultations, and telemedicine. It ensures accurate digital health records and patient information management for effective diagnosis and treatment.
  4. How can radio and television help students in their studies?
    Answer: Radio and television broadcast lectures and educational programs, providing accessible learning resources and enhancing understanding, especially in remote areas.
  5. What is telemedicine?
    Answer: Telemedicine is the use of ICT to provide medical services remotely using electronic communication.
  6. What role does ICT play in business and commerce?
    Answer: ICT enables digital tools and platforms to promote products, engage with customers, analyze market data, and facilitate online transactions.
  7. What is a smartphone?
    Answer: A smartphone is a multipurpose mobile device with features of both a phone and a computer, including a rechargeable battery, touch screen, internet access, and apps for communication, media, and gaming.
  8. Name some default apps which come preinstalled on smartphones?
    Answer: Phone, camera, calendar, mail, photos, clock, maps, messages, web browser, music, Google Play Store, and App Store.
  9. What is Bluetooth? What is its use?
    Answer: Bluetooth is a short-range wireless technology that connects devices within 30 feet to send messages and share files.
  10. What are the different parts of a smartphone screen?
    Answer: Touchscreen for display and input, status/notification bar, app icons, navigation buttons, and the home screen.
