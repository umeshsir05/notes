1. What is self-confidence?
Answer :-
Self-confidence is a feeling of trust in your abilities to perform a particular task. Every person has some strengths and some weaknesses. People having self-confidence not only have faith in their strengths but also have conviction that they can deal with their weaknesses and overcome them.

2. Write some cultural factors that influence the build-up of self-confidence among individuals.
Answer –
Cultural Factors Influencing Self-Confidence:
1. Cultural Norms: Conservative cultures with many restrictions can limit personal expression, lowering self-confidence.
2. Gender Stereotypes: Cultures that treat girls as inferior often damage their self-worth and confidence compared to those raised in more equal, supportive environments.

3. What are some social factors that build one’s confidence?
Answer –
1. Upbringing: A loving and supportive family environment nurtures self-worth.
2. Societal Influences: Encouragement from peers, teachers, and community builds social confidence.
3. Media: Positive role models in media inspire belief in oneself.
4. Belief System: Strong values and faith provide inner strength and courage.

4. How personal hygiene can boost one's self-confidence?
Answer -
Maintaining personal hygiene or cleanliness boosts self-confidence by enhancing health, appearance, and improved social and personal interaction.

5. What role does gender stereotyping play in the development of self-confidence?
Answer –
At times certain cultures discriminate against girls and consider them as inferior to male children. Girls from such cultures will surely have lower levels of self-esteem and self-confidence than girls coming from a non-discriminatory culture.

6. What is the importance of finding your unique qualities and using them to develop your confidence?
Answer:
Finding your unique qualities helps you understand your strengths and abilities. When you recognize what makes you special, you begin to trust yourself more. Using these qualities in your daily life builds self-confidence because you feel capable and proud of who you are.

7. How can you overcome set-backs with self-confidence?
Answer:
You can overcome setbacks with self-confidence by staying positive, learning from your mistakes, and not giving up. Believing in your ability to improve and try again helps you move forward. Self-confidence gives you the strength to face challenges and grow from them.

8. Can you yourself build your own confidence or do you need someone else to do it for you?
Answer:
You can build your own confidence by setting small goals, celebrating achievements, and learning from experiences. While others can support or motivate you, real confidence comes from within. It grows when you believe in yourself and keep trying, even when it’s hard.
