Chapter – 18

Health and Safety at Work


1. Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). Tick (✔) the correct answer.

1. 1. Presence of dangerous chemicals at a workplace exposes workers to which type of hazard? a. Chemical hazard b. Biological hazard c. Ergonomic hazard d. Physical hazard Correct Answer (to tick): a. Chemical hazard ✔ 2. Infected blood in a hospital presents what type of hazard to the hospital workers? a. Chemical hazard b. Biological hazard c. Ergonomic hazard d. Physical hazard Correct Answer (to tick): b. Biological hazard ✔ --- [File: 1000346847.jpg] 3. Which type of hazard can cause skin irritation, illness, or breathing problems? a. Psycho-social hazard b. Biological hazard c. Ergonomic hazard d. Chemical hazard Correct Answer (to tick): d. Chemical hazard 4. Consumption of which of the following substances can be harmful to health? a. Fruits and vegetables b. Milk c. Tobacco and alcohol d. Cereals and pulses Correct Answer (to tick): c. Tobacco and alcohol 5. Which of these does not come under the safety hazards category at the workplace? a. Slipping hazards b. Tripping hazards c. Chatting hazards d. Repetitive movements Correct Answer (to tick): c. Chatting hazards (This is not a standard occupational safety hazard.)
