III. Short Answer Questions.

1. What is Rapid Typing tutor?
Answer-
Rapid Typing tutor is a free and open source software (FOSS) designed to learn typing skills on the computer. You can use it and share it with others without incurring any cost. The Rapid Typing tutor can be used by both beginners and advanced level learners.

2. What are the main components of the Rapid typing tutor window?
Answer-
Main components of the Rapid Typing Tutor window are- 1. Title Bar – Shows software name and mode tabs (Current Lesson, Statistics, Editor).
2. Options & Control Buttons – Minimize, Maximize, Close, and Settings.
3. Toolbar – Allows lesson selection and control.
4. Text Panel – Displays the typing text.
5. Status Bar – Shows tips, progress, and volume.
6. Virtual Keyboard – Assists in touch typing.

3. Why looking at the keyboard to find keys is not allowed in touch typing?
Answer-
Looking at the keyboard during touch typing is not allowed because it reduces speed, accuracy, and focus. It breaks the typing flow and prevents the development of muscle memory. Touch typing relies on practicing key positions by feel, allowing the eyes to stay on the screen for better efficiency and fewer errors.

4. How will you download Rapid Typing tutor on your computer?
Answer-
We can download the latest version of the Rapid typing software by visiting the website rapidtyping.com. The website also has a number of typing tools, games and other features to make learning to type easy and fun.

5. What is the base position for your fingers while typing on the
(a) keyboard (b) numeric keypad?
Answer-
Base Position for Fingers While Typing:
(a) Keyboard (Alphabetic Part):
• The base position is called the "Home Row".
• Place your fingers as follows:
o Left hand: A - S - D - F
o Right hand: J - K - L - ;
o Both thumbs rest lightly on the Spacebar.
(b) Numeric Keypad:
• The base position is:
o Index finger on 4,
o Middle finger on 5,
o Ring finger on 6,
o Thumb rests on the 0 (zero) key.

6. What is the use of the Options button on the Rapid Typing tutor's main window?
Answer-
The options button displays a list of options to control the Rapid Typing’s settings. Others are minimize, maximize and close buttons with their usual functions.

7. What does the Lesson editor help us to do?
Answer-
Lesson editor allows you to add, insert, change, and delete lessons, group them in courses, create new courses and lessons and review the corresponding metrics.

8. How can we view the results of a typist lesson?
Answer-
To view the result of a typist that you have already completed, go to the tutor’s main window and select the Students Statistics tab button from the title bar. The statistics screen will open on your computer. Here you can review your overall statistics and progress both in graphical and table view.

9. What are the various units for measuring the typing speed?
Answer-
The various units for measuring the typing speed are-
Unit Description Formula
WPM The number of words typed in one minute period of time. WPM = (Words without errors + Words with errors) / Time spent in minutes.
Net WPM the WPM without words with errors. Net WPM = WPM - (Words with errors / Time spent in minutes).
CPM The number of characters typed in a one minute period of time. CPM = (Characters without errors + Characters with errors)/Time spent in minutes
Net CPM The CPM without characters with errors. Net CPM = CPM - (Characters with errors / Time spent in minutes)
KPM The number of keystrokes in a one minute period of time. KPM = (Keystrokes without errors + Keystrokes with errors)/Time spent in minutes.
Net KPM The number of keystrokes in a one minute period of time. KPM = (Keystrokes without errors + Keystrokes with errors)/Time spent in minutes.
10. How can we calculate the net typing speed of a person?
Answer-
Net typing speed is the number of correct words typed per minute. It is calculated by subtracting errors from total words typed, then dividing by time in minutes. Formula: Net WPM = WPM - (Words with errors / Time spent in minutes)

11. What is meant by accuracy in typing? How is the typing accuracy calculated?
Answer-
Typing accuracy refers to how correctly a person types without making errors. It is the percentage of correctly typed characters or words compared to the total number of characters or words typed. High accuracy means fewer mistakes while typing.

12. What is the importance of maintaining rhythm in timing?
Answer-
In touch typing techniques, the typing rhythm is very important . Typing Rhythm means the keystrokes should come at equal intervals . To control the constant typing speed, the slowdown indicator is used.
