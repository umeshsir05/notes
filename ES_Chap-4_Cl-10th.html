IV. Short Answer Questions:

1. What is the meaning of a sentence?
Answer-
A sentence is a group of words that makes complete sense, expresses a thought or idea, and has a subject and a predicate.

2. What are articles? Explain the difference between the use of articles 'a' and 'an'.
Answer-
Articles are words used before nouns to define whether the noun is specific or unspecific. 'A' is used before words that begin with a consonant sound (e.g., a book), and 'an' is used before words that begin with a vowel sound (e.g., an apple).

3. What are the eight parts of speech? Explain each with a suitable example.
Answer-
The eight parts of speech are as follows:
1. Noun
A noun is the name of a person, place, thing, or idea. Example: Ravi is reading a book.
2. Pronoun
A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun to avoid repetition. Example: He is a good boy.
3. Verb
A verb is a word that shows action or state of being. Example: She runs fast.
4. Adjective
An adjective describes or modifies a noun or pronoun. Example: It is a beautiful flower.
5. Adverb
An adverb modifies a verb, adjective, or another adverb. Example: He speaks softly.
6. Preposition
A preposition shows the relationship between a noun (or pronoun) and other words in a sentence. Example: The book is on the table.
7. Conjunction
A conjunction joins words, phrases, or clauses. Example: I like tea and coffee.
8. Interjection
An interjection expresses strong emotion or sudden feeling. Example: Wow! What a lovely painting!

4. Write a paragraph not exceeding 150 words on the topic 'Growing Threat of Environmental Pollution'.
Answer-
Environmental pollution is one of the most serious problems the world is facing today. It refers to the contamination of the natural environment, including air, water, and soil. Pollution is mainly caused by human activities such as industrialization, deforestation, and the use of plastic and chemicals. Air pollution leads to respiratory problems, while water pollution harms aquatic life and affects drinking water. Soil pollution reduces agricultural productivity. If pollution continues to increase, it will have dangerous effects on the health of humans, animals, and plants. To control pollution, we must use eco-friendly products, plant more trees, reduce waste, and spread awareness. Every individual must take responsibility to protect the environment for future generations.

5. What are conjunctions? Explain with the help of a few examples.
Answer-
Conjunctions are words used to join words, phrases, or sentences. They help in making complex and meaningful sentences. Examples include:
and (joins similar ideas): I bought apples and bananas.
but (shows contrast): He is poor but honest.
because (shows reason): I am late because of the traffic.
